# Ethium

The **whole plateau** is Ethium — not a settlement on the high ground, but a **huge ancient city** that once covered it.

## What travellers see

- From Fallcrest: only a faint pale shelf on the horizon when the haze lifts — not individual ruins
- On the plateau: foundations, roads, walls, and rubble in every direction — the city’s bones under grass and vine
- **Royal castle:** ruins on the **largest hill**, ringed with cliffs — the grandest structure still standing

## What the party knows (published chapters)

- Tobbs (Ch. 2): the plateau **was** the city; vast, abandoned, stones waking
- On arrival (Ch. 4): they are walking through Ethium itself, not approaching a single ruin
- They see the castle from the plateau but **do not yet know** it was the **king’s home**
