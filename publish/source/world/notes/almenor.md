This is the **country** where the adventures begin — on the **Axirian peninsula**, south of the central mountains.

- **Capital:** Sunfall (★ on the peninsula map)
- **Ruler:** the King of Almenor
- **Fallcrest:** northern frontier town; last proper settlement before the wild hills and Ethium ruins
