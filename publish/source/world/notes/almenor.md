This is the **country** where the adventures begin — on the **Axirian peninsula**, south of the central mountains.

- **Capital:** Sunfall (★ on the peninsula map) — south of Almenor, on the river
- **Ruler:** the King of Almenor
- **Fallcrest:** northern frontier town (○ on the map) at the mountain foothills — Almenor’s border with the unclaimed range; last proper settlement before the wild hills and Ethium ruins

**Pronunciation:** Al-men-or (as on the peninsula map: **ALMENOR**).
