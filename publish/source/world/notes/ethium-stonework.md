# Ethium stonework

Building stone from the Ethium plateau — towers, roads, shrines, pools, and carved work.

## Appearance

- **Colour:** light, near-white
- **Feel:** hard as granite, smooth as marble
- **At rest:** no blue veins, glow, or colour in the rock itself

## When blue appears

Blue light or veins in **stonework** only show when **Ethium Stones** activate it — placed in sockets, spoken to with the old language, or brought near carved channels built to channel stone-magic.

The **stones** themselves (the egg-sized fragments) may show blue veins when waking or in use. That is not the same as the building stone.

## In prose

- Ruins on the plateau: pale, colourless blocks unless a stone has been used nearby
- Pools and water features may hold their own old magic in the water; the surrounding stone stays pale until stones are set in the rim sockets
