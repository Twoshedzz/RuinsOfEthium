# Ruined manor — cellar layout

**No custom map file.** Played on preprinted tabletop boards at the table. Geographic detail is not vital for the novel — three connected areas are enough.

## Setting

Outside Fallcrest: an ancient manor house, destroyed in the orc attack long ago. The cellars beneath remain intact. Sergeant Murgaddin sent the party here after the Blue Moon brawl — the Hammers were operating from it.

Stairs descend from the ruined ground floor into the cellars.

## Three areas (in order)

### 1. Entrance — Hammers

- First cellar chamber
- **Hammer thugs** guard the approach
- Rough, makeshift camp feel — crates, torchlight, muddy footprints

### 2. Damp chamber — Hammers and wizard

- Deeper in: damp air, **glowing mushrooms**
- A **mage** (Rulden) and more **Hammers** planning around a table
- Party defeated them here
- **Mage had an Ethium stone**
- **Loot:** magical shortsword, gold, identify scroll, healing potion
- **Dave** copied the **Sleep** spell from the mage's spellbook

### 3. Sealed tomb — skeletons

- Beyond sealed doors the party broke through
- **Family tomb:** four sarcophagi
- Skeletons erupted when disturbed (family curse or dark past — deliberately unclear)
- More gold after the fight

## Aftermath

- Party returned to **Murgaddin** with a **letter** seized from the lair (Maelis Varn / Valnaran family — see session play log)
