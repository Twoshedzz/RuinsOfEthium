## Core idea

The **Ethium pool** is not passive — it draws creatures, reacts to stones, and changes over time. Safe hub with pressure.

**Healing:** once per rest, each PC regains **1d6 HP** from the pool. Lingering may cause whispers, dream-flashes, or corruption marks.

**Combat pulses** (initiative count 0): heal 1d4 in 10 ft · advantage on next roll · 1d6 force · Ethium wisp appears.

## Encounter — Night ambush at pool

**Enemies:** 2 Hammer Bruisers, 2 Silent Knives, cloaked observer.

Hammers: loud, frontal, may be Ethium-sick (blue veins). Silent Knives: flank, steal stones, disengage. Unstable alliance — hired to seize stones.
