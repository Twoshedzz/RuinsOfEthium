# Campaign bible — Chronicles of Ethium

Living reference for canon. Update after each session.

## The book

- **Title:** Chronicles of Ethium
- **Subtitle:** The Adventures of Thorn Axehand and Friends
- **Audience:** Readers aged 12+ (Peter's son and friends at the table)
- **Basis:** Real D&D sessions — battles, decisions, and silly ideas really happened
- **Tone:** Fantasy adventure novel; humour welcome; expand descriptions, don't invent major plot

> *"Some treasures are buried beneath the earth. Others are found in the friends who choose to search for them."*

## Active party

**At the table and in the novel (so far):** Thorn Axehand, Loki (Thorn's dog), Derek, Dave, Nibbles McPicklepants.

**Not yet in play:** **Willow Starbloom** — Peter's daughter's character; will join the campaign later. See [`characters/pcs.md`](characters/pcs.md) and [`characters/party-backgrounds.md`](characters/party-backgrounds.md).

## Plot summary so far

### Published (website)

1. **Prologue** — Thorn, Loki, Derek, Dave, and Nibbles on the North Road. Goblins growing bolder. Name **Ethium** not yet spoken.
2. **Ch. 1** — Goblin ambush; rescue **Mara**; goblin boss and Ratbag; map for Nimozaran; Blue Moon promised.
3. **Ch. 2** — **Fallcrest**; Sergeant Murgaddin; Blue Moon feast; homunculus; **Tobbs** and Ethium map; Hammers brawl; **Gang of Four** named.

### Played, not yet published ([`sessions/session-03/what-happened.md`](sessions/session-03/what-happened.md))

4. **Ruined manor** — Hammer hideout in cellars; mage with Ethium stone; Dave learns **Sleep**; family tomb skeletons; letter from **Maelis Varn** (Valnaran / Gang of Four) ordering seizure of Mara's map.
5. **Road to Ethium** — wolves; axe beaks; **cloaked watcher** following.
6. **Waterfall** — split climb; goblins and bugbears; Nibbles downed, healed by Derek; Dave's Sleep and Fire Bolt.
7. **Ethium plateau** — healing shrine; **dragon** foreshadowing; half-goblin; distant **castle**; wolf riders.
8. **Ruined tower** — Hammer camp; undead past adventurers; rescue **Garnel Stoneblender**; maul trap; **undead wizard** at healing **pool**; Garnel activates pool with Ethium stones.

## Key locations

- **North Road** — goblin activity; Mara ambushed here
- **Fallcrest** — Nentir Vale frontier town; Hightown / Lowtown
- **Blue Moon Alehouse** — Mara's meeting place
- **Ruined manor** — Hammer hideout outside Fallcrest (orc-war ruin, intact cellars)
- **Ethium plateau** — cliffs, waterfall access, shrine, ruined tower, distant castle
- **Ruined tower dungeon** — spiral stairs, Ethium stonework, healing pool
- **Sunfall** — capital of Almanor; Gang of Four base
- **Ethium** — ancient civilisation; stones reawakening
