Use these as short, punchy descriptions you can drop in:
### The Dragon (Foreshadowing)
A distant shape cuts across the clouds. At first a bird… then too large. Wings like torn shadows. A long tail trailing behind. It circles once… then disappears into the haze.
(If they watch longer:)
Something falls from its mouth far in the distance… tumbling into the hills below.

### Goblin Remains (Optional discovery later)
The smell hits before the sight. A goblin—torn clean in half. No tracks. No struggle. Just… dropped.
(Clue reinforcement: *they are not the top predators here*)

### Castle on the Hill
Through a break in the trees, you glimpse it— A blackened stone castle on a distant hill. One tower leans. Another has collapsed entirely. Something moves along the walls… but too far to make out.

### Goblin Wolf Riders (Non-combat)
You hear them before you see them—low growls and snapping jaws. A pack of goblins on wolves races across a distant ridge. Fast. Organised. Hunting something… not you.
(They ignore the party unless provoked—builds scale)

A small stone shrine sits half-sunken into the earth. The roof has collapsed, but the central altar still stands. Strange, smooth stones are set into its surface… faintly warm to the touch.
