Raised in a travelling fair by his parents who were both famous psychic mediums, Merlin had a happy childhood, though showed no sign of any magical abilities.

This changed when he was 17, when after a show in the Sword Mountains the troop was attacked by a large and vicious clan of Kobolds, aided by what appeared to be an Orc Sorcerer, all of which were marked by pentagrams carved roughly into their foreheads.

With the camp almost overrun completely Merlin’s powers manifested suddenly, as the young mage panicked and sent out an uncontrolled stream of firebolts and lightning which killed many of the Kobolds but unfortunately also injured a number of the travelling performers and set light to much of their tents and baggage.When the flames died down the attackers had gone, though his fellow travellers made it angrily clear Merlin would have to leave the company, afraid as they were he would lose control again in the future. Furious at this treatment and still highly confused about his newfound abilities, it took Merlin some time to realise that amongst the dead there was no sign of his parents’ bodies either dead or alive.

Merlin vowed to find out where his parents had been taken, and to take revenge on the creatures who had perpetrated the attack. He left the camp that night, however 15 years later he is still pursuing the creatures who had caused such chaos in his life.

Merlin has been forced to search further and further all over the realm in his quest, and has sought to better his magical abilities through study with other sorcerers and practitioners (as he has come to believe that it is only through mastery of enchantment and occultism that he can find his parents).

Over this time he has joined a number of groups of adventurers, and even fought in mage battalion at one time, to make a living but also to aid access areas of danger where he believes his parents may be held. Merlin has gained a reputation as a ruthless and volatile wizard, who is quick to attack potential enemies even when negotiation may lead to combat being avoided.
