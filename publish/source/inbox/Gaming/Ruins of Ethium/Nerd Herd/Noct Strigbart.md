A Dittyflock of the Strigbart clan. Dittyflocks are a rite of passage for young, dwarven bards. They are sent out into the world to spread Dwarven history and to claim personal victories, including their first kill. Honour is determined by distance covered and by risk taken. Noct is the son of Urnal Strigbart, who was a boy during the conquering of Citadel Felbarr. Urnal wrote The Fall of Felbarr, as part of his Dittyflock, which made him famous among all dwarfs. To add to Noct’s pressure, the last two dwarfs sent out as Dittyflocks, never returned.

Like most dwarfs, Noct is stubborn and slow to forget. Dwarfs respect tradition and place high value on social standings. They are determined, loyal, and decisive in battle. Every action is taken with the reputation of the clan in mind. Drinks are never refused and every opportunity to slay an orc is relished and savoured.
