
## 1. Arrival at Fallcrest

### Player description

> “Stone walls rise ahead, weathered but strong. Banners snap in the wind above the northern gate, and beyond them you hear the sound of hammers, voices, and rushing water far below.
> 
> This is Fallcrest — a town rebuilt on old ruins, standing at the edge of safer lands.”

The guards at Knight’s Gate stop the party briefly. Before questions get awkward, **Mara steps forward**.

> “These are the ones who saved me. From goblins on the road.”

The mood changes immediately.

---

### DM info

- The guards have already heard reports of goblins near the roads
    
- The party are treated as **respected newcomers**, not suspects
    
- This establishes early that their actions matter
    

---

## 2. Exploring the Town (Lowtown Focus)

### Player description

> “Inside the walls, Fallcrest feels busy but worn. Porters haul crates up steep paths. Merchants call out prices. Children run through muddy streets. Above it all, cliffs rise sharply, crowned by sturdier buildings.”

The party have time to:

- Find food
    
- Find lodgings
    
- Explore
    
- Ask questions
    

The **Blue Moon Alehouse** stands out as lively and welcoming.

---

### DM info

- Don’t overwhelm them with locations
    
- Anchor early play around **Blue Moon Alehouse**, **Nentir Inn**, and **Septarch’s Tower**
    
- NPCs are friendly but cautious — Fallcrest has been hurt before
    

---

## 3. Reward for Rescuing Mara

### Player description

Mara thanks the party personally.

> “I wouldn’t be alive without you. I made a promise to someone in town — but I won’t forget what you did.”

She arranges:

- A modest coin reward
    
- Free drinks / food at the Blue Moon
    
- A good word put in with town authorities
    

---

### DM info

- Keep rewards **meaningful but not magical**
    
- The real reward is **access and trust**
    
- Mara remains important — she is the connective tissue between events
    

---

## 4. The Map & Tobbs Quickfoot

### Player description

Later, Mara grows serious.

> “Before the goblins took me, I was carrying something important.  
> A map. I still need to deliver it.”

She asks the party to accompany her to meet **Tobbs Quickfoot**, a halfling assistant to the wizard **Nimozaran**.

In a quiet back room, Tobbs unrolls the map — ancient, marked with runes and symbols.

> “This shouldn’t exist anymore…”

---

### DM info

- The map shows the **ruins of Ethium**
    
- Ethium is believed looted and overrun
    
- Recently, **Ethium Stones** have begun to reactivate
    
- Nimozaran wants samples for study
    

Tobbs explains:

- Someone knew about the map
    
- Someone raced Mara to Fallcrest
    
- They failed — and are angry
    

---

## 5. The Watching Homunculus

### Player description

While at the Blue Moon, local children laugh and tease the innkeeper.

> “Oi! Your roof’s got a silly statue again!”

When anyone looks:

- There’s nothing there
    
- Just tiles and chimneys
    

Later — quietly — someone notices movement.  
A small stone-like shape, crouched high above, watching.

Then it’s gone.

---

### DM info

- This is a **homunculus familiar**
    
- It belongs to a **gang-aligned mage**
    
- It is spying on the party and Tobbs
    
- It avoids confrontation at all costs
    

If noticed:

- It flees
    
- Leaves unease, not answers
    

---

## 6. The Gang of Four Strike (Blue Moon Brawl)

### Player description

As the night wears on, rough figures enter the alehouse.  
They watch. Whisper. One steps closer.

> “Funny thing. Maps are dangerous. Best hand it over.”

An insult. A spilled drink. A shove.

The room erupts.

---

### DM info

- The attackers are **The Hammers**, backed by the wider **Gang of Four**
    
- Their goal is **the map**, not murder
    
- The fight is loud, messy, and non-lethal
    

---

### Bar Fight Encounter (very light stats)

**Hammer Thug (x3–4)**

- Low AC, low HP
    
- Fists, clubs, tankards
    
- Shove, grapple, intimidate
    

**Hammer Bruiser (x1)**

- Higher HP
    
- Focuses on map carrier
    
- Backs down if outmatched
    

**Tactics**

- Knockouts, not killing
    
- Retreat when guards arrive
    

---

## 7. Aftermath & Revelation

### Player description

Outside in the cold air, one gang member lies groaning.

With pressure, he spits out:

> “We were paid to grab the map. That’s it. Didn’t even know what was on it.”

He reveals:

- The gang was **racing Mara to Fallcrest**
    
- Their base is nearby
    
- Others may already be heading to Ethium
    

---

### DM info

The **Gang of Four**:

- Based in the capital **Almanor**
    
- Four allied guilds, unstable but coordinated
    
- Recently hired by **unknown magical agents** (Axir — secret)
    

The local cell operates from:

- An **old ruined manor** outside Fallcrest
    
- Pre-dates the orc/goblin sack
    
- Has a **cellar / dungeon beneath**
    

---

## 8. The Choice (End of Session)

Present the choice clearly.

### OPTION A — **Deal with the Gang**

The ruined manor lies just outside town.  
If left alone, the gang may:

- Regroup
    
- Alert others
    
- Follow the party to Ethium
    

**Ruined Manor Encounters**

- Lookouts
    
- Traps
    
- Gang lieutenant
    
- Evidence of who hired them
    

**Enemies**

- Hammer Thugs
    
- Valnaran agent (tiefling)
    
- Guard dog or rats in cellar
    

---

### OPTION B — **Press On to Ethium**

Leave at first light.  
Race whoever else is interested.

**Road Encounters (pick 1–2)**

**Goblin Scouts (x2–3)**

- Watching the road
    
- Avoid fair fights
    

**Axe Beak Herd**

- Territorial, fast
    
- Can be avoided, scattered, or fought
    
- Great outdoor combat set-piece
    

**Foreshadowing**

- Strange lights
    
- Crystals reacting
    
- Signs others have passed through
    

---

## 9. What the Players Know (Clear State)

- Ethium is waking up
    
- Someone powerful wants it studied — or controlled
    
- Criminal gangs are involved
    
- They are already being watched
    
- Their choices matter
