## Part 1 — The ruined manor

Outside Fallcrest: an ancient house destroyed in the orc attack; cellars intact. Sergeant Murgaddin reported the Hammers operating from it.

**Cellar layout:** three areas only (played on preprinted boards — no map file). See [`../../world/maps/ruined-manor-cellar.md`](../../world/maps/ruined-manor-cellar.md).

1. **Entrance** — Hammer thugs
2. **Damp mushroom chamber** — mage (Rulden) + Hammers; Ethium stone; Dave learns **Sleep**; loot (shortsword, gold, identify scroll, healing potion)
3. **Sealed family tomb** — four sarcophagi; skeletons erupt; more gold

- Returned to Murgaddin victorious with a **letter** from the lair

### Letter (to the manor mage, from Maelis Varn)

> **Rulden,**  
> The arrangement stands. You will prove your worth now.  
> A courier travels the north road within the next tenday, bearing a map recovered from the outer ruins of **Ethium**. It must not reach Fallcrest.  
> Seize it. Kill the courier if needed, but bring the map intact.  
> You are not the only ones searching. Others have begun to take interest in the old places. This cannot be allowed to spread.  
> Deliver the map to my agent at the river crossing. You will be paid in full upon confirmation.  
> Do not fail me again.  
> — **Maelis Varn**, *Third Hand of the Valnaran family*

- Murgaddin: linked to **Gang of Four** — four criminal guilds united in **Sunfall** (capital); power reaching north
- Party rested, then set off for **Ethium** (Fallcrest-to-Ethium map)

## Part 3 — The waterfall climb

- Tobbs had directed them to this route in Fallcrest
- Treacherous stone path behind the waterfall to reach the Ethium plateau
- Party split: **Nibbles and Derek** — steeper right-hand route; **Thorn, Dave, Loki** (and rest) — longer safer path
- Goblins fired arrows from above; **bugbears** came down to attack
- **Dave:** Sleep spell — stopped the arrows effectively
- **Thorn:** dramatic fight with bugbear behind the waterfall
- **Nibbles:** fought a bugbear valiantly, fell; **Derek** healed him twice; on the second recovery Nibbles slew the bugbear
- Party topped the cliff; fleeing goblin caught by **Dave's Fire Bolt**

## Part 4 — Across the Ethium plateau

- Found collapsed **shrine** — healing bestowed on the party
- **At shrine / overland** (all occurred):
  - Distant **black dragon** shape; something falls from its mouth far away
  - **Half a goblin** found — torn clean in half, no tracks (dragon drop)
  - **Castle on a hill** glimpsed in the distance — blackened stone, leaning tower
  - **Goblin wolf riders** on a distant ridge (non-combat; hunting something else)
- Heading for **ruined tower** Tobbs said held magic

## Part 5 — Ruined tower (Hammer camp)

- Camp at tower — attackers appear as **bandits** until defeated; **Hammer pendants** found
- Snipers in trees with bows during fight
- Party gained supplies; found **spiral stairs** descending
- Ethium tunnels: well-dressed stone, ornate subtle carvings; dark, damp

### Alcove — old adventurers

- Grimey corner: sludge, debris from past looters decades ago
- Glint of metal in rubble → investigating awakens **three zombies** (old dead adventurers; Ethium magic or necromancer below?)

### Garnel's room

- Orc brute + Hammers forcing captive **gnome** to read runes on walls
- **Garnel Stoneblender** — kidnapped in Sunfall; tries to help party when attacked
- Party **rescued Garnel**; he knows something of Ethium

### Trap & boss

- Collapsed staircase: **swinging maul** over plank across chasm — party avoided it
- **Pool room:** dead wizard at a desk, corrupted by Ethium investigation; became **undead**
- Tough fight with wizard + zombies; on death wizard **exploded** with Ethium energy — **Nibbles knocked down**
- **Garnel** helped configure pool with stones found — **healing pool** activated (water from a source elsewhere)
