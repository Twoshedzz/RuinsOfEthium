The North Road had always been a dangerous place.

Travellers spoke of wolves in the forests, bandits on lonely stretches of road and stranger things lurking amongst forgotten ruins. Yet during recent months even seasoned merchants had begun whispering about goblins growing bolder than ever before. Caravans vanished. Couriers disappeared. Something was changing in the wild lands beyond civilisation.

Most people chose safer roads.

Four young adventurers chose the North Road instead.

There was Thorn Axehand, a fearless half-orc who believed courage could solve almost any problem. Walking faithfully beside him padded Loki, a loyal guard dog with an excellent nose and absolutely no fear.

Travelling alongside them came Derek, a sturdy dwarven cleric whose calm judgement often prevented the others from getting themselves into even greater trouble.

Dave, the youngest member of the party, was a copper dragonborn wizard with an unusual talent. While other wizards studied elegant spells of force and fire, Dave somehow managed to transform one of the most famous spells in the world into a barrage of flying potatoes. Nobody—not even Dave himself—could quite explain why.

Finally there was Nibbles McPicklepants, a dragonborn rogue whose curiosity was almost as dangerous as the monsters they would soon encounter. If there was treasure to be found, Nibbles would usually discover it first.

None of them knew each other particularly well.

None of them had any idea what awaited them.

And none of them had ever heard the name...

**Ethium.**
